Tut is South Sudanese man living in Kakuma Refugee camp. He has been in the camp for more than 10 years.
He began his primary school in the camp and finally completey his secondary school within the camp.

Tut started working with Danish Refugee Council (DRC) from 2019 to date. He understands the value and the need to give
back to the society and it is the main reason has to why he is still working with DRC. He is a computer instructor who happens
to have more than 2 years experience.

Tut is passionate and optimistic about education because he was wants to acquire more knowledge and skills in order to be equiped to
give better to the society. As a result, he is currently pursuing Diploma in Information Technology at Masinde Muliro University
of Science and Technology. Tut is not only a student but a student with big dreams and potential to make such dreams come true.

Tut is planning to back to South Sudan after his Diploma to help develop and make his country a better place to be.
