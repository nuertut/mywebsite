Tut experienced a lot of challenges due to war between North and South Sudan before the independence in July 2011. Due to war, there was no proper education in the country. Tut manage to flea to the neighbouring country Kenya in search for better education in order to get knowledge that can help him make his country a better place for everyone. Tut has successfully completed his secondary education and his now pursuing diploma in Information Technology at Masinde Muliro University of Science and technology. He is currently working with Danish Refugee council in Kakuma Refugee camp
